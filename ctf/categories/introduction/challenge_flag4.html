Flag 4

Difficulty: Medium • Category: Introduction

Not everything is as it seems. Sometimes you're given a hint, like an IP address or a common phrase, but the actual flag you need to submit is encoded or transformed in a specific way.

In this challenge, you’ll be given a **key** and a clue that looks like regular text. It’s your job to apply the key and figure out what the transformed flag should be.

Example: If the clue is `one-ninety-two-dot-one-sixty-eight`, and the key tells you to convert words to leetspeak, you’d submit something like `SKT{0n3-n1n3ty-tw0-d0t-1-s1xty-8}`.

This challenge will train you to spot encoded flags and use the right transformation. Watch the format and apply the key!
