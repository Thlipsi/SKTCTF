Flag 1

Difficulty: Easy • Category: Introduction

Flags can be hidden in many ways, but they always follow the same format:
They start with SKT{ and end with }.
Inside the braces, words are separated by hyphens, and the text may include numbers or mixed case.

Example: SKT{1m-A-Fl49}
